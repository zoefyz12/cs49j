Complete the Verb verb class which represents a verb in English. This is related to, but different from, one of your homework problems.

You are provided with a constructor that takes a String containing the verb as as a parameter . You are also given a getVerb() method which returns the verb.

Provide this method:

getGerund()- returns the gerund form of the verb. A gerund is a verb form ending in "ing".

Here are the rules for forming the gerund. (The rules are actually a little more complicated than this, but just use these rules.)

  • If the verb ends in an "e", remove the "e" and add "ing"
    • past tense of hope is hoping
  • If the verb is three characters long and ends in a consonant, double the consonant and then add "ing" . The vowels are aeiouy.
    • past tense of hop is hopping
  • In all other cases, add "ing" to the end of the verb

Note: only use one return statement.
